## Core Product vs. Core Moat

This addendum clarifies the distinction between what Velolume **sells** (the product) and what Velolume **builds** as defensible infrastructure (the moat).

---

## The Core Product (What We Sell)

> "Turn your videos into money and insights with zero manual work."

Creators don't buy infrastructure or protocols. They buy **outcomes**:

- **Making more money** — Monetize every product, not just the featured item
- **Saving time** — Zero manual linking, tagging, or shop management
- **Understanding what works** — Data-driven insights for better content

---

## The Core Moat (What We Build)

> "Structured creator data that compounds in value."

The protocol for AI-discoverable creator data is not the product—it's the defensible infrastructure that emerges from serving creators well:

- Every video processed = more structured data
- Structured data = AI-queryable creator commerce intelligence
- When AI agents need creator data, we have the largest dataset

---

## The Strategic Hierarchy

| Layer | Focus | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Layer 1** | What We Say (Product Positioning) | "Monetize your content automatically. Get smarter about what works." |
| **Layer 2** | What We Do (Core Capability) | Convert video → structured data → revenue + insights |
| **Layer 3** | What We Own (Defensible Moat) | The largest structured creator commerce dataset, queryable by AI systems. |

---

## Why This Distinction Matters

### Leading with "we're building a protocol for AI discovery":
- Creators don't understand it
- No immediate revenue
- Depends on uncertain AI ecosystem evolution

### Leading with "make money from your videos automatically":
- Creators get it immediately
- Revenue from day one
- Protocol builds quietly as a byproduct of usage

---

## Implications for Go-to-Market

1. **Messaging focuses on creator outcomes**, not infrastructure
2. **AI-readiness is a future benefit**, not the primary value prop
3. The six value propositions remain valid; AEO is positioned as "future-proofing"
4. **For investors:** The moat narrative explains long-term defensibility

---

## Summary

**Sell the product. Build the moat.**

The product gets creators in the door. The structured data they generate becomes the defensible asset that makes Velolume hard to displace when AI-mediated commerce arrives.
